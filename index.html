What is ActiVT about?

ActiVT is a student-oriented platform that allows Virginia Tech Students to setup on campus activities and find others to join their activities.

How can I join?

If you are interested in finding activities, head over to the login page to setup an account. Check out the how-to page after to learn the basics.
