About Section

What is ActiVT?

ActiVT is a platform that allows Virginia Tech students to easily find activites happening on campus.

The Goal

Finding others with similar interests can be tough, so we thought, "Why not make a platform that allows students to find activities happening on campus easily?" And that is exactly what we did here. ActiVT's smooth layout allows users to easily find activities happening on campus, save those activites, and then show up to said activities.

Features

Post section that allows users to easily find activies occuring on campus and when.

Profile section to allow users to view that activities that they have liked and the ability to share those posts with friends.

Easy post creation menu that allows anyone to create a post and promote their activity.
